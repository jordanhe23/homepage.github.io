Jordan He, FCAS, MAAA

Hi, I am Jordan. I am a credential property & casualty actuary living in Seattle, Washington.

I work as a Senior Actuary at the Texas Windstorm Insurance Association and the Texas Fair Plan Association. I lead all actuarial and analytical initiatives for residual market property insurance within the state of Texas, focusing on wind and hail coverages along the Texas coast.

Before my time at Texas Windstorm, I spent 11+ years as a consulting actuary at PwC's Risk Modeling Services. In various roles, I served a diverse range of insurance clients, including traditional primary insurers, reinsurers, captives, large corporates, public entities and self-insured risk pools. My work encompassed loss reserving, insurance pricing, capital modeling, simulation modeling, experience rating as well as risk financing.

I am self-taught in coding. In my spare time, I enjoy exploring data science and machine learning projects to keep up with the latest trends in the analytics space.
